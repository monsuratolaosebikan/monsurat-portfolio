About Me

I'm a 4th year mechanical engineering student at Northeastern University who found her passion in coding and 3D printing. Don’t let my major fool you, I’m a mechanical engineer by degree but a software engineer at heart. Although I enjoy building physical products, what I have come to learn is that I enjoy building digital products that improve our experiences in the physical world more. I've worked at GE Transportation as an Advanced Manufacturing (3D Printing) Co-op and AholdUSA as Front-End Developer Co-op. I have experience with Node, Angular, React Native, Arduino and 3D Printing. In my free time I like making clocks from interesting materials, pop up cards, and reading on my kindle.
